Innovation Project Engineering Process




Innovation Project Timeline




Brainstorming


We identified several issues related to different hobbies and art forms to find a manageable problem to solve. We organized our brainstorming into an excel spreadsheet to keep track of our ideas, our selection criteria, and the characteristics and merits of each. After that, we created a list of problems that we could tackle and narrowed it down to one based on originality, feasibility, and degree of impact.




List of Ideas


Together we brainstormed multiple ideas. Here is a list of them:


  • Goggles that provide you with the best angle to shoot a basket
  • Meter in a soccerball that tracks the force of kick, speed, spin
  • Glasses that help you find your golf ball
  • Heart monitor that helps me control my speed to make me a better endurance swimmer
  • Kata scoring system / device / app for competitions
  • Kata trainer
  • Goggles that translate notes off sheet music to the keys on a keyboard / piano
  • Attachment for guitar to help beginners ease the pain of playing
  • Music scoring device / app
  • Shoes to help you dance
  • Projector that helps with dancing by showing where feet should go
  • App to help practice drama lines
  • Training aid for knowledge-based games, such as chess and Scrabble
  • Hiking board game
  • Cooking safety
  • Eye safety from screens app / game
  • Music scoring device / app
  • App that helps kids transition from picture books to novels by generating images
