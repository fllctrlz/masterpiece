Teaching Youth About STEM and FLL as Part of Toronto Police Service's Project Engage416


On July 17, 24, and August 8, we joined the Toronto Police Service in an anti-gang initiative called "Engage416." The goal of the "Engage416" initiative is to inspire youth to participate in STEM through robotics and provide youth with alternative opportunities. We taught programming and how to complete some SUPERPOWERED missions to youth in the Smithfield neighbourhood of Toronto.





Preparing for FLL Ontario 2023 MASTERPIECE Season Kickoff


We spent the month of September preparing presentations and learning different coding languages for both EV3 and Spike Prime. We learned meaningful skills, such as learning how to give proper presentations.




Presenting at the FLL Ontario 2022 SUPERPOWERED Season Kickoff


On October 1, 2023 we presented to 294 people at the FLL 2023 MASTERPIECE season kickoff. There we taught programming skills, both EV3 and Spike Prime.
