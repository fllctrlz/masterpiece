Teaching disadvantaged youth about STEM at a police-run summer camp


On July 17 and 24, and also August 8, we joined the Toronto Police Services in an anti-gang intiative called "Engage416." The goal of the "Engage416" initiative is to inspire children about STEM before gang activity begins to affect them.




Preparing for FLL Ontario 2023 MASTERPIECE Season Kickoff


We spent the month of September preparing presentations and learning different coding languages for both EV3 and Spike Prime. We learned meaningful skills, such as learning how to give proper presentations.
